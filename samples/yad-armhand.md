## Actions

### Attacks

| Attack          | Range          | To Hit | Damage | Damage Type      |
| --------------- | -------------- | ------ | ------ | ---------------- |
| Unarmed Strike  | 5 ft.          | +8     | 1d10+5 | Bludgeoning or Force |
| Pugilist Weapon | varies         | +8     | 1d10+5 | by weapon (Fisticuffs die) |
| Javelin (melee) | 5 ft.          | +8     | 1d10+5 | Piercing; Silvered |
| Javelin (thrown)| 30/120 ft.     | +8     | 1d10+5 | Piercing; Silvered, Slow |

A Pugilist weapon is any Simple Melee weapon or improvised weapon; you use the
Fisticuffs die (**1d10**) in place of its normal damage.

The Javelin is a Simple Melee weapon, so it counts as a Pugilist weapon whether
you strike with it or throw it. That means you use the Fisticuffs die (1d10 in
place of its normal 1d6), giving 1d10+5 either way, and Haymaker works with it.

Unarmed Strikes and improvised-weapon attacks can deal Force damage instead of
their normal type (Moxie-Fueled Fists) — this does **not** apply to the Javelin,
which is a real weapon, not improvised. With Haymaker you can spend 1 Moxie Point
to deal maximum damage on a hit (and regain the point on a hit).

### Unarmed Strike (rules text)

You make a melee attack that involves using your body to deal one of the following effects:

- **Damage.** You make an attack roll against the creature, and on a hit, you deal 1 + STR Bludgeoning damage.
- **Grapple.** The target must succeed on a Str./Dex. (it chooses which) saving throw (DC = 8 + Prof. Bonus + Str.) or it has the Grappled condition.
- **Shove.** The target must succeed on a Str./Dex. (it chooses which) saving throw (DC = 8 + Prof. Bonus + Str.) or you can either push it 5 ft. away or cause it to have the Prone condition.

#### Fisticuffs (Fisticuffs Die: 1d10)
You have mastery over combat styles that use Unarmed Strikes and Pugilist weapons.
Pugilist weapons are Simple Melee weapons and improvised weapons.

While you are unarmed or wielding only Pugilist weapons, wearing Light or no armor,
and not wielding a Shield, you gain:

- **Bonus Unarmed Strike.** You can make an Unarmed Strike as a Bonus Action.
- **Fisticuffs Die.** You can roll **1d10** in place of the normal damage of your
  Unarmed Strike or Pugilist weapons.
- **Improved Improvisation.** You can use the mastery properties of improvised
  weapons. All improvised weapons count as having the **Sap** mastery property for
  you. **Sap:** if you hit a creature with the weapon as part of the **Attack
  action** on your turn, that creature has **Disadvantage on its next attack roll**
  before the start of your next turn. (Applies only to actual improvised weapons —
  not Unarmed Strikes and not the Javelin or other real weapons.)

#### Iron Chin
While you are wearing Light or no armor and not wielding a Shield, your base Armor
Class equals 12 + your Constitution modifier = **15 AC**.

#### Moxie (5 Max Moxie Points)
You have **5 Moxie Points**. Spend them to fuel certain Pugilist features. Expended
points are unavailable until you finish a Short or Long Rest, at the end of which
you regain all of them. You start knowing three Moxie features:

- **Brace Up.** Bonus Action, expend 1 Moxie Point: roll your Fisticuffs die and
  gain Temporary Hit Points equal to the number rolled **+ 11** (your Pugilist
  level + Constitution modifier). These Temp HP vanish if any remain after 10
  minutes.
- **One-Two Punch.** Expend 1 Moxie Point to make two Unarmed Strikes as a Bonus
  Action.
- **Stick and Move.** Bonus Action, expend 1 Moxie Point: make an Unarmed Strike
  and take the Dash or Disengage action.

#### Bloodied But Unbowed (2nd level)
When you take damage, you can take a Reaction to regain all your expended Moxie
Points. If you are Bloodied when you take this Reaction, you also gain Temporary
Hit Points equal to four times your Pugilist level = **32**. These Temp HP vanish
if any remain when you finish a Short Rest.

Once you use this feature, you can't use it again until you finish a Short or Long
Rest.

#### Swagger Streak (2nd level)
When you fail a Strength, Dexterity, Constitution, or Charisma check, you can
expend a Moxie Point to attempt to swagger your way to success. Roll **1d10** and
add the number rolled to the ability check, potentially turning it into a success.
If the ability check still fails, you regain the expended Moxie Point and can't use
this feature again until you finish a Short or Long Rest.

#### Heavy Hitter (3rd level)
When you hit a creature with an Unarmed Strike, you can use both the Damage and
your choice of the Grapple or Shove option.

### Pugilist Subclass — Squared Circle

> *Drop and Lock Your Foes.* Pugilists who fight in the Squared Circle know the
> fastest way to win a fight is to force a submission.

#### Groundwork (3rd level)
You gain the following benefits.

- **Compression Lock.** When you start your turn with one or more creatures
  Grappled, you can deal **1d10+5** Bludgeoning damage (one roll of your Fisticuffs
  die + Strength modifier) to each creature you have Grappled. No action required.
- **Inescapable.** When a creature makes a saving throw or ability check against
  your grapple or shove DC, you can expend 1 Moxie Point to give that roll
  Disadvantage.
- **Stop and Drop.** When you hit a creature with an Unarmed Strike and don't use
  a mastery property as part of the attack, you can use both the Grapple and Shove
  option.

#### Muscle Mass (3rd level)
You gain **Expertise in Athletics**.

#### Ability Score Improvement — Pugilist 4 (Feat: Athlete)
See the Feats section for Athlete.

#### Dig Deep (4th level)
You can use a Bonus Action to dig deep. For 10 minutes, you have Resistance to
Bludgeoning, Piercing, and Slashing damage and you ignore the effects of
Exhaustion levels less than 6.

Once you use this feature, you can't use it again until you finish a Long Rest
unless you gain 1 Exhaustion level (no action required) to restore your use of it.

#### Extra Attack (5th level)
You can attack twice instead of once whenever you take the Attack action on your
turn.

#### Haymaker (5th level)
When you make an attack with an Unarmed Strike or Pugilist weapon, you can expend
1 Moxie Point to swing with wild abandon. On a hit, regain the expended Moxie
Point and you deal maximum damage with that attack.

#### Moxie-Fueled Fists (6th level)
Whenever you deal damage with your Unarmed Strike or an attack with an improvised
weapon, it can deal your choice of Force damage or its normal damage type. This is
a passive option on the damage you already deal — it is not an action and costs no
Moxie.

#### Meat Shield (6th level)
While you have a creature Grappled, you gain Half Cover against attacks made by
creatures you aren't Grappling. When a creature you aren't Grappling misses you
with an attack roll, you can take a Reaction and expend 1 Moxie Point to force that
creature to make the same attack with a new attack roll against a creature you are
Grappling.

#### Down But Not Out (7th level)
When you use your Bloodied But Unbowed while you are Bloodied, you can add a bonus
to the damage of your Unarmed Strikes and attacks with Pugilist weapons for the
next minute. This bonus equals your Constitution modifier + the number of levels of
Exhaustion you have = **3 + your current Exhaustion levels**. Once you use this
feature, you can't use it again until you finish a Long Rest.

#### Ability Score Improvement — Pugilist 8 (+2 Strength)
Increased Strength by 2 (can't raise a score above 20).

### Species Traits — Goliath

- **Creature Type:** You are a Humanoid.
- **Size:** Medium.
- **Speed:** 35 ft.
- **Giant Ancestry:** You are descended from Giants. Your chosen boon is **Hill's
  Tumble (Hill Giant):** when you hit a Large or smaller creature with an attack
  roll and deal damage to it, you can give that target the Prone condition. Usable
  **3 times** (equal to Proficiency Bonus) per Long Rest.
- **Large Form:** As a Bonus Action, if you're in a big enough space, you can
  change your size to Large. This lasts 10 minutes or until you end it (no action).
  While Large, you have Advantage on Strength checks and your Speed increases by 10
  feet. Once per Long Rest.
- **Powerful Build:** You have Advantage on any ability check you make to end the
  Grappled condition. You also count as one size larger when determining your
  carrying capacity.

### Feats

- **Athlete**
  - Strength increased by 1 (already included in your Strength score).
  - **Climb Speed** equal to your Speed (35 ft.).
  - **Hop Up.** When you have the Prone condition, you can right yourself with only
    5 feet of movement.
  - **Jumping.** You can make a running Long or High Jump after moving only 5 feet.
- **Lucky**
  - **3 Luck Points**, regained after a Long Rest.
  - **Advantage.** When you roll a d20 for a D20 Test, spend 1 Luck Point to give
    yourself Advantage on the roll.
  - **Disadvantage.** When a creature rolls a d20 for an attack roll against you,
    spend 1 Luck Point to impose Disadvantage on that roll.

### Background — Wayfarer

Wayfarer was chosen for the Lucky feat. Its ability increases are applied as
**+2 Strength and +1 Constitution** (see How the Ability Scores Are Built).
