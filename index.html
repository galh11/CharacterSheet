Key Combos & Tactics

The Vex Cycle: Hit with Blood-Wood Fang to activate Vex, granting Advantage for an easy Sneak Attack.
Shadow Assassin: Cast Darkness on yourself. Your Shadowflame Sight gives you Advantage against blinded enemies inside.
Sticky Duelist: Hit an enemy and spend a charge for Booming Blade. The target is now "stuck," and Vex gives you Advantage.
Hit and Run Master: 1. Action: Attack (Vex) + Nick. 2. BA: Cunning Action (Disengage). 3. Free Attack: Second Trick.
Nova Strike: 1. BA: Whisperstep (Teleport + Advantage). 2. Action: Sneak Attack (stack all damage). 3. Action Surge: Attack again with Advantage from Vex.
