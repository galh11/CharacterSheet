- **Fisticuffs** — unarmed strikes use the **1d10** Fisticuffs die + STR (force or bludgeoning).
- **Iron Chin** — while in light/no armor & no shield, base **AC = 12 + con_mod**; DEX does not apply. → sets AC (relational).
- **Moxie** — resource pool (see Resources); fuels maneuvers.
- **Bloodied But Unbowed** — benefit while bloodied (per-rest feature).
- **Swagger Streak** — 1d10 momentum die feature.
- **Heavy Hitter** — extra impact on unarmed hits.
- **Dig Deep** — grit/push feature (Restore Dig Deep on rest).
- **Extra Attack** — attack twice with the Attack action (`set extra-attacks 1`).

- **Moxie-Fueled Fists** — spend Moxie to add force damage / empower unarmed. → action toggle.
- **Down But Not Out** — activate to stay up / recover.

**Squared Circle (subclass):**
- **Groundwork** — Compression Lock (1d10+STR bludgeoning grapple strike), Inescapable, Stop and Drop.
- **Muscle Mass** — **expertise in Athletics**. → skill expertise.
- **Meat Shield** — soak/protect ally reaction.

**Goliath (species):**
- **Giant Ancestry — Hill's Tumble** — knock prone rider; uses = **proficiency bonus (3)**, recharge long rest.
- **Large Form** — 1/long rest: become Large, **+10 ft speed** and **advantage on STR checks** while active. → toggle/resource.
- **Powerful Build** — **advantage to end the Grappled condition**; carrying capacity counts as one size larger.

**Feats:**
- **Lucky** — Luck points = **proficiency (3)**, recharge long rest.
- **Athlete** (half-feat) — +1 STR; **climb speed = walking speed**; stand from prone costs less.
- **Ability Score Improvement** — +2 STR.
- **Wayfarer ASI** — +3 CHA (offset by −3 bonusStat).
